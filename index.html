Functional Eigen-Grasping Using Approach Heatmaps

Malek Aburub, Kazuki Higashi, Weiwei Wan, Kensuke Harada

Robot Automation Letter (RA-L), 2025

Webpage / Paper / Video

This paper addresses the challenge of how to approach objects to functionally grasp them. It focuses on utilizing the object's characteristics to synthesize the grasp and opt out of using human demonstrations.
